REFRESH YOUR DAY

We believe that beer is for sipping. The best beers are made for your enjoyment. Made for those that savor every taste and those who drink to experience the bursts of flavors. For those searching for authenticity and excitement in their novelty beers. We take honor in our Japanese origin and work to brew our beers with veracity. To the dedicated alcohol enthusiasts looking for the perfect refreshment, pour yourself a drink that is made with originality. Drink the beer that will refresh your day.

FLAVOR LISTING

Yuzu Sun

Yuzu is a citrus fruit popular in Japan. The flavor is similar to a combination of lemon and grapefruit. Included in a 12-pack with Matcha Sun and Nashi Sun.

Sakura Seltzer

This drink incorporates the abundance of Cherry Blossom in Japan and the popularity of peaches in Japan. This flavor is a seltzer as they are more fruity and less for beer.

Matcha Sun

This drink contains Hinode beer as the base with a splash of vodka, matcha tea, and peach juice. The drink is sold in a can with a matcha tea packet to pour into your drink. Included in a 12-pack with Yuzu Sun and Nashi Sun.

Hinode Dawn

This drink is a non-alcoholic version of Hinode Beer. Drinking is very popular in Japan and we want those who prefer non-alcoholic beverages to partake in the enjoyment of Hinode Beer.
