About Us

Founded in 2022, this brand is authentic and true to its Japanese culture. The brand reflects and celebrates Japanese drinking traditions. This brand emanates cheerfulness through the enjoyableness of the beer itself.

Our product is a malt beer that is fermented with rice, making our beer gluten-free. It is a lower alcohol percentage, as expected with a malt beer, at 3.8% alcohol content. This beer is made to be enjoyed so users can appreciate the different flavors.

Our promise to consumers is to provide a beer that is true to its roots and tastes good. We want consumers to enjoy a drink that is sippable and easy to appreciate. We promise to stick to the authenticity that is Hinode beer.
